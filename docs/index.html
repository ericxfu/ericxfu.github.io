Group Intro Meeting

August 21, 2023

First group meeting to discuss our expectations of the project and areas of expertise as well as brainstorming areas of interest and problems to solve.

Problem Area Proposal

September 18, 2023

Met with the professors to pitch our agreed upon problem area which was generalized to “Problems in the kitchen”. We were tasked with narrowing the scope via talking to stakeholders to determine which issues are most common in the kitchen.

Selecting a Specific Problem

September 25, 2023

After conducting surveys that reached over 100 participants and interviewing a wide range of people such as a chef, a parent, cooking enthusiasts and those who hate the kitchen, the selected problem was to focus on difficulty with the access and storage of ingredients.

Defining a Problem Statement

October 2, 2023

After further discussion, the problem statement was defined as “It is difficult to store, organize and access cooking ingredients, specifically spices”. To begin to tackle how we would solve this issue, we formulated a list of criteria and constraints which can be found. link to wherever we are putting the criteria and constraints of the problem

Generating Design Concepts

October 10, 2023

Based on the agreed upon criteria and constraints, we began to brainstorm the different mechanisms that would be involved such as the storage, dispensing, measuring, refill, and UI method.

Preliminary Design Presentation

October 16, 2023

Our first iteration of designs were created in which the top 3 were chosen and fleshed out and can be seen in some gallery here. Each of these 3 designs were ranked according to their ability to meet the various criteria and we determined that the third design aligned best with our goals for the project. Patent research was also done which showed that the concept itself is not unique but certain features such as multi-ingredient dispensing and automated refilling could be patented. Sensitivity Analysis

November 11th, 2023

Using feedback from project advisors, a sensitivity analysis was completed on the design selection process. It was found that the selection process was sensitive to two factors: speed and accuracy. Further discussion about the results determined that the current trajectory of the project was satisfactory.

Software Flow Chart

November 20th, 2023

A software design flowchart was created outlining the system logic and flow from user input to performing an action of refilling or dispensing.

Key Concept Integration

November 18, 2023

With initial designs of electrical and mechanical components, an initial prototype was constructed and tested. This proof of concept verified the functionality of our refill and dispensing mechanisms. Show media from construction day
