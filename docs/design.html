In 2023, five brothers set out to revolutionize the culinary game forever.

Countless hours of brainstorming, deliberating, head-scratching, finally gave birth to a sleek, skillful, sexy, and serious yet friendly design to solve one of mankind's greatest problems.

Cooking.

Our creation seeks to bridge the gap between Michelin-star level meal seasoning and humble home cooking in one fell swoop.

By housing a different spice ingredient in each compartment, as well as providing the capability to dispense and refill any spice of choice on command to your heart's content, our device is sure to be an invaluable addition to any culinary hopeful's arsenal.
